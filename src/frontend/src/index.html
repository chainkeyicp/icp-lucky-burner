How It Works

The Basics

  • Every day one winner is drawn from all ticket holders for the Daily Prize.
  • Tickets cost 0.1 ICP each · maximum 10 tickets per wallet per day.
  • Winnings are sent automatically to your principal — no claiming needed.
  • The more tickets you buy, the higher your chance of winning.

Mystery Pools

In addition to the Daily Prize, three mystery pools accumulate over time. After each daily draw, the canister rolls the dice — a mystery can drop any day, without warning.

🔮
Small Mystery
25% chance per day
Drops on average every ~4 days
✨
Medium Mystery
10% chance per day
Drops on average every ~10 days
💎
Large Mystery
3% chance per day
Drops on average every ~33 days

Each mystery has a separate winner draw — you can win the daily prize and a mystery on the same day. Mysteries only drop after reaching a minimum pool size.

Fairness & Guarantees

  • Winner selection uses ICP's native on-chain randomness — threshold BLS signatures, not manipulable by anyone.
  • All payouts are recorded on the ICP ledger with a block index you can verify publicly.
  • 10% of every ticket funds the canisters via the Cycles Minting Canister — ICP is burned from supply in the process.
  • Smart contracts run fully on-chain — no server, no admin can alter results or access funds.
